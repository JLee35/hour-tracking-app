Welcome to the Scout Lake Construction Payroll Application

Use this application to log hours for yourself and your employees.

The process of this application is as follows:

  • Enter employee name, job site, and week ending date
  • Enter general hourly information for each day (time in/out, lunch start/stop)
  • Enter specific hourly information for each day (individual tasks and time associated)
  • Review, sign, and submit timecard

You can save your progress during any step of this application by clicking Save & sign out or erase the current timecard by clicking Delete. After signing and submitting, the timecard will be emailed to your project manager.


Click Start new to create a new timecard for yourself or an employee.

Click Continue existing to pick up where you left off with an existing timecard.
