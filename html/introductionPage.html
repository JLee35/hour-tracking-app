Welcome to the Scout Lake Construction Payroll Application

Use this application to log hours for yourself and your employees.

The process of this application is as follows:

  • Enter employee name, job site, and week ending date
  • Enter general hourly information for each day (time in/out, lunch start/stop)
  • Enter specific hourly information for each day (individual tasks and hours associated)
  • Review, sign, and submit timecard
Please feel free to complete each section in any order, navigating between sections will not erase your current progress.

Timecards must be completed in one session, your progress will not be saved if you close the application or select Sign Out. The timecard must be signed and submitted in order to be reviewed by your project manager. If the timecard has been submitted successfully, you will be prompted with a success message followed by the option to create a new timecard or sign out.


Click Start new to create a new timecard for yourself or an employee.
